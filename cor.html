Colours

13. Portuguese Colors

Since the colors in Portuguese are adjectives, they must agree with the noun in gender and number. Colors are also placed after the noun they describe, not before the noun as in English.

red vermelho violet violeta
pink rosa brown marrom
orange laranja dark brown marrom escuro
yellow amarelo black preto
green verde gray cinza
blue azul white branco
light blue azul claro gold dourado
purple roxo silver prateado
