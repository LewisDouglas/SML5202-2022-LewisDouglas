Portuguese Future Tense (Futuro do Presente)

The futuro do presente is the simple future tense, which in English is expressed by the verb 'will' + and infinitive.

all verbs
-ei -emos
-ás -eis
-á -ão

It is important to pay attention to how fazer changes to far before adding the endings.

Ela fará a aula. She will make the class Vocês chegaão a sua casa? Will you drive to your house ?
