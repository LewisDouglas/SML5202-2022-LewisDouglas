With this website you will be able to learn all about the Portuguese language and its culture.

Learn with online lessons, games, intetractive quizes and excercises that have been specifically enginered to give the most interactive online tuition to our students

Portuguese is a romance language, spoken globally by over 250 million poeple on three continents. Mostly spoken in Brazil, Portugal, but also Mozambique, Angola and Guinea-Bissau to name a few countries.
As such, there is an array of variations in these native 'dialects' spoken globally, however, the basic rules remain the same throughout. On this site you can learn the basics to the Portuguese language, and will be able to have the fundamental building blocks required to take your language learning to the next level.

These lessons will focus mainly on the European and Brazilian variations of the Portuguese language, as they are the most popular.
