My Shortbread Recipie

Ingredients

  • 125g unsalted butter, softened
  • 55g caster sugar, plus extra to finish
  • 180g plain flour

Method

  1. Preheat the oveb to 190C
  2. Beat the butter and the sugar together until smooth
  3. Stir in rhe flour to get a smooth paste. Turn on to a work surface and gently roll out unitl the paste is 1cm thick
  4. Cut into shorbread rounds or fingers and place onto a baking tray. Sprinkle with caster sugar and chill in the fridge for 20 minutes
  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until golden-brown. Set aside to cool on a wire rack

Nutritional Information Per Serving

  • 2g fat
  • 25 calories per buscuit;
  • 1g protein
  • 10g sugar
  • 0.2g saturated fat
