Books and online activities

  • Duolingo - A popular language learning platform with interactive grammar exercises and lessons
  • Collins Portuguese Dictionary - A comprehensive online dictionary with grammar and usage notes
  • Portuguese Verbs: Essential Grammar and Reference - A book that provides a comprehensive overview of Portuguese verb conjugation and usage
  • The Complete Portuguese Grammar - A comprehensive guide to Portuguese grammar, with exercises and examples to help consolidate learning
  • Collins Easy Learning Portuguese Verbs - A book that provides a clear and easy-to-understand guide to Portuguese verb conjugation, with over 250 irregular and regular verbs
  • Lingoda - An online language school that offers Portuguese grammar and conversation classes with native speakers
  • Memrise - A language learning platform with interactive grammar exercises and lessons, including a course on Portuguese grammar and vocabulary
  • PortuguesePod101 - An online language learning platform that offers Portuguese grammar lessons, vocabulary lists, and cultural notes
  • Babbel - A language learning app that offers Portuguese grammar and conversation classes with native speakers
  • PortugueseClass - A website that offers Portuguese grammar lessons and exercises, with additional resources for learners of all levels
